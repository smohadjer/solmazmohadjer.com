Outreach Activities

My outreach activities range from developing educational videos and training school teachers to working closely with policymakers to inform the disaster risk reduction decision-making process and strengthen the resulting policies. Some of my activities are highlighted below.

  • Founder of ParsQuake Project, since 2011
  • Member of European Geosciences Union Outreach Committee, since 2020
  • Science adviser, EU Parliament, Brussels, Belgium, 11/2019
  • Delegate at the UN Forum on Science, Technology and Innovation, NY, U.S.A., 5/2019 (Read more)
  • Development and evaluation of paired teaching earthquake education videos, since 2016
